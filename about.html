About AI & Lotto Tools

Welcome to AI & Lotto Tools! Our mission is to provide simple, fun, and useful online tools for everyone to enjoy.

Our Animal Face AI

Our Animal Face Test uses advanced machine learning (TensorFlow.js) to classify photos of cats and dogs. We process all images locally in your browser, meaning your photos are never sent to a server, ensuring your privacy.

Our Lotto Generator

Looking for lucky numbers? Our Lotto Generator uses a fair random algorithm to help you pick numbers for your next ticket. It's simple, fast, and fun.
