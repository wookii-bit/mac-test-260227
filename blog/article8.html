8. Stress and the Brain: Understanding Its Impact

Stress is a natural response to challenges and threats. Chronic stress, however, can have a significant impact on our physical and mental health.

The Physiology of Stress

When we experience stress, our body releases stress hormones like cortisol and adrenaline. These hormones can affect our heart rate, blood pressure, and immune system.

The Psychology of Stress

Chronic stress can lead to mental health issues like anxiety and depression. It can also affect our cognitive performance and ability to cope with daily life.

Managing Stress

Stress management is essential for our well-being. Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing and meditation to reduce stress and improve your health.
