7. Emotional Intelligence: Why It Matters

Emotional intelligence (EQ) is the ability to recognize and manage your own emotions and understand the emotions of others.

Self-Awareness and Self-Regulation

Self-awareness is the foundation of emotional intelligence. It involves understanding your emotions and how they affect your behavior. Self-regulation is the ability to manage your emotions and respond to them in a healthy and constructive way.

Empathy and Social Awareness

Empathy is the ability to understand and share the feelings of others. Social awareness is the ability to recognize and respond to the emotions of others in a social context.

Cultivating Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence can be developed through practice and intention. Practice mindfulness and self-reflection to increase your self-awareness and self-regulation.
