12. Cognitive Biases: How Our Brains Trick Us

Our cognitive processes can have a significant impact on our behavior and beliefs. Understanding cognitive biases can help us make more informed decisions and avoid self-deception.

Foundations of Cognitive Biases

Cognitive biases are mental shortcuts that our brain takes to simplify information processing and decision-making.

Reducing Cognitive Biases

Reducing cognitive biases involves recognizing our own biases and developing strategies to overcome them.

The Power of Autonomy

Developing a sense of autonomy and self-reliance can help us resist cognitive biases and make more authentic decisions.
