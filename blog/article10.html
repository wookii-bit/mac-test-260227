10. Active Listening: Improving Your Communication

Communication is the foundation of building strong relationships and successful careers. Active listening is a key skill that involves paying attention to the message and responding in a healthy and constructive way.

The Foundations of Active Listening

Active listening involves paying attention to the speaker, clarifying information, and responding with empathy and understanding.

Empathy and Social Awareness

Empathy is the ability to understand and share the feelings of others. Social awareness is the ability to recognize and respond to the emotions of others in a social context.

Cultivating Active Listening

Active listening can be developed through practice and intention. Practice mindfulness and self-reflection to increase your self-awareness and self-regulation.
