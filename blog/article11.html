11. Sleep Psychology: Why Your Brain Needs Rest

Sleep is essential for our physical and mental well-being. It's a natural response to challenges and threats.

The Foundations of Sleep

Sleep cycles involve different stages of sleep, including REM and non-REM sleep. Each stage plays a key role in our physical and mental health.

The Psychology of Sleep

Sleep can affect our cognitive performance, emotional regulation, and memory consolidation. Chronic sleep deprivation can lead to mental health issues like anxiety and depression.

Managing Sleep

Sleep management is essential for our well-being. Practice good sleep hygiene and relaxation techniques like deep breathing and meditation to improve your sleep quality.
